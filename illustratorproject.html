幸福

Prompt: Create a set of conditions by which you derive a series of paths using Illustrator, and realize a physical manifestation of these conditions using the laser cutters in the Brown Design Workshop.

Inspired by traditional Chinese paper cutting and its association with luck and happiness, I created and layered paper cut-outs to depict an image of what happiness looks like to me: a family eating dinner together. The four layers were drawn in Illustrator and cut with a laser cutter before being sewn together with red thread. I originally wanted many layers with many scenes associated with things I am grateful for, and I wanted the piece to be able to stand on its own, like a book cracked open, so that the viewer could see each individual page. However, due to time constraints, I only created the four pages, but in the future, I would like to explore using more layers, as well as paper of different textures and transparencies.
